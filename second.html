The Daily Schedule

  1. Day 1: Take a bus from Kathmandu to Pokhara. Check into the hotel and explore the local market in the evening.
  2. Day 2: Visit the Tal Barahi Temple and enjoy the scenic views of the Annapurna range.
  3. Day 3: Take a day trip to the Phewa Lake and enjoy the boat ride.
  4. Day 4: Explore the Davis Falls and Gupteshwor Cave. In the evening, visit the Lakeside area for shopping and dining.
  5. Day 5: Take a bus back to Kathmandu and end the trip.
